Information is retained as reasonably necessary for promotion administration, legal compliance, recordkeeping, fraud prevention, and dispute handling.

Promotions are not intended for individuals under 18. We do not knowingly collect information from minors.

6. Privacy Requests

To request access, correction, or deletion where applicable, contact support@purple-flare.com.
